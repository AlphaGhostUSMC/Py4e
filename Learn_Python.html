Operator Precedence Rules

Highest precedence rule to lowest precedence rule:

  • Parentheses are always respected
  • Exponentiation (raise to a power)
  • Multiplication, division, and remainder
  • Addition and subtraction
  • Left to right
 
  >>> x = 1 + 2 ** 3 / 4 * 5
  >>> print(x)
  11.0
  >>>
