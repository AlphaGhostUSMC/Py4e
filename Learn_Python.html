Indentation

  • Increase indent after an if statement or for statement (after :)
  • Maintain indent to indicate the scope of the block (which lines are affected by the if/for)
  • Reduce indent back to the level of the if statement or forstatement to indicate the end of the block
  • Blank lines are ignored - they do not affect indentation
  • Comments on a line by themselves are ignored with regards to indentation
